Our Values

How we think about building Tidus.

Three principles that shape every product and routing decision we make.

Do the Right Thing

When the easy path and the right path diverge, we take the right one. This means being honest about what Tidus can and can't do, even when it costs us a sale. Trust is the only durable moat.

In practice: We document limitations. We surface routing confidence. We tell customers when a competitor might serve them better.

Put the Client First

Our customers are spending real money on AI APIs, trusting us to optimize it well. Every routing decision Tidus makes is a direct impact on their bill. We take that seriously.

In practice: We publish cost savings transparently. We give customers visibility into routing decisions. We make it easy to override Tidus when they know better.

Innovative but Stable

AI model APIs change constantly. Tidus has to stay current — but not at the expense of systems our customers depend on. API contracts are sacred. Breaking changes get migration paths and notice.

In practice: We version our APIs. We run new routing models in shadow mode before promoting them. We communicate changes before they land.
