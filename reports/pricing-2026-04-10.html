ℹ️ How to read these prices
Input priceCost per 1M tokens you send to the model — your prompt, system instruction, conversation history, and any context you attach. Longer prompts or large document uploads drive this cost up.
Output priceCost per 1M tokens the model generates — every word in its response. Verbose answers, long code completions, and streaming replies all accrue output cost. Output is typically 3–5× more expensive than input.
$/1M tokensIndustry-standard unit. To estimate a task: (prompt tokens ÷ 1,000,000) × input price + (response tokens ÷ 1,000,000) × output price. A typical 500-word prompt (≈ 700 tokens) + 500-word reply costs under $0.01 on most models.
